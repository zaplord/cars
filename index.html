BMW 3 Series

The BMW 3 Series is a compact executive car manufactured by BMW since 1975. It's known for its sporty handling, premium interior, and efficient engines. Available in sedan, wagon, and Gran Turismo body styles.

  • Engine options: 2.0L turbo, 3.0L turbo
  • Starting price: $35,000
  • Fuel economy: 26-36 MPG

BMW 5 Series

The BMW 5 Series is an executive car that has been manufactured by BMW since 1972. It offers luxury, performance, and advanced technology in a sophisticated package.

  • Engine options: 2.0L turbo, 3.0L turbo, 4.4L V8
  • Starting price: $55,000
  • Fuel economy: 23-34 MPG
